The Majestic Ants

Everything About Ants

Ants are considered to be one of the most common insects as you can find them almost anywhere. They are social insects so they live in large colonies ranging from a few thousand up to millions of ants per colony. There are over 12,000 known ant species to date.

As a whole, ant populations are stable. However, particular ant species like the Black bog ant and the Narrow-headed ant are endangered

    Features

    Most ants are omnivores. To live in variety of habitats, ants have modified their behavior and physical features so most ant characteristics can vary wildly between species. Ants can lift 10-50 times their body weight. They do not have ears so they hear by feeling the vibrations in the ground through their feet. Lastly, ants benefit ecosystems by dispersing seeds, pollinating plants, and improving the quality of soil.
